Ticket Types

Your primary responsibility is managing the ticket system. We operate with two primary ticket categories.

General Ticket

Created by community members for support inquiries, moderator assistance, or reporting issues.

Roster Ticket

Application channel for users seeking to join Void Esports. Follow structured onboarding workflow.

Protocol: All ticket responses begin with professional greeting and age inquiry.

Mod Responsibilities

Warn / Report

Use *warn user-id reason in #staff-commands for minor violations.

LOA

Use /loa command with duration and reason for absences.

Weekly Metrics

  • 10 Tickets - Process support tickets
  • 400 Messages - Community engagement
  • 5 Modlogs - Documented actions
  • 8 Hours - Minimum activity
Performance below standards triggers review.
